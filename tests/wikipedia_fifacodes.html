List of FIFA country codes

  • Article
  • Talk
  • Language
  • Watch
  • Edit

FIFA assigns a three-letter country code (more properly termed a trigram or trigraph[1]) to each of its member and non-member countries. These are the official codes used by FIFA and its continental confederations (AFC, CAF, CONCACAF, CONMEBOL, OFC and UEFA) as name abbreviations of countries and dependent areas, in official competitions.

FIFA, IOC and ISO differences edit

Main article: Comparison of alphabetic country codes

For the majority of countries, FIFA codes are the same as both the International Olympic Committee country codes used for the Olympic Games and the ISO 3166 country codes. When these two are different, FIFA mostly chooses the IOC code, sometimes the ISO code. For three countries, FIFA uses a distinct code. Codes that differ from the FIFA code are coloured in light red.
